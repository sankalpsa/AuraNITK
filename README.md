**Aura** is a premium, real-time campus dating and networking platform designed for students across all premier institutes. It brings together tech-native students to find meaningful connections, study partners, or that special someone within their university ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Why Aura?

In a demanding academic environment, finding the right connection should be easy, secure, and personal. Aura bridges the gap with features tailored specifically for the modern campus lifestyle.

### 🛡️ Verified & Secure
*   **Universal Access**: Sign up with any valid university email.
*   **Real-time verification**: Instant OTP verification ensures every profile belongs to a legitimate student.
*   **Safety First**: Built-in reporting and blocking mechanics to keep our community respectful and secure.

### 💖 Smart Matching
*   **Interest Alignment**: Our algorithm calculates your **Match %** based on shared interests, branch compatibility, and year of study.
*   **University Priority**: Prioritize matches from your own campus while exploring global connections.
*   **Green & Red Flags**: Express your personality through customized flags to find someone who fits your vibe.

### 💬 Premium Chat Experience
*   **Real-time Interaction**: Instant messaging powered by Socket.io.
*   **Multimedia Support**: Send photos and **Voice Messages** for a more personal touch.
*   **Smart Indicators**: Typing indicators and read receipts with blue double-checks.
*   **Rich Previews**: Integrated image previews and reply functionality.
